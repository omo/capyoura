Welcome to Capyoura!

Capyoura or "Cap Your Addiction" is Google Chrome extension that keeps you away from a popular addiction; Endless Web Browsing.

With capyoura, the browser extension watches what and how many pages you browse, and "CAP" when you seems get addicted.

How to use it?

To use Capyoura, You need download and install the extension, then login this site to register your favorites and addictions.

Because we use Google accounts, NO extra signup/registation is required.
