Say Good-bye to the Web addiction!

Capyoura (pronounced かぴゅーら) or "Cap Your Addiction" is Google Chrome extension that keeps you away from Endless Web Browsing; One of the worst addictions ever.

Capyoura's browser extension watches what and how many web sites you browse, and "cap" them when you seems get addicted.

How to try it?

To try Capyoura, You need download and install the extension. then login this site.

We use Google accounts, so no extra signup/registation is required.

After registering your favorites/addictives and their tolerable level, Capyoura automatically start watching you browsing Web.

Privacy Concern?

Because Capyoura extension only tell the server when you visits your capped sites, NO confidential information is sent unless it is registered to your cap list.
